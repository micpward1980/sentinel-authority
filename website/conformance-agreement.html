ODD Conformance Determination Terms and Conditions

This Conformance Agreement ("Agreement") governs the relationship between Sentinel Authority ("SA") and the organization ("Applicant") seeking ODD Conformance Determination (ODDC) for autonomous systems. By submitting a certification application, Applicant agrees to this Agreement.

Critical Understanding

ODDC attests that a system operates within declared boundaries with non-bypassable enforcement at the time of determination. ODDC is not: regulatory approval, safety certification, product certification, a guarantee of performance, insurance, or warranty. Applicant remains solely responsible for system behavior, regulatory compliance, and all consequences of operation.

1. Scope of Determination

SA will evaluate and determine conformance based on:

  • ODD Specification: The Operational Design Domain as declared by Applicant
  • ENVELO Compliance: Runtime enforcement architecture meeting ENVELO requirements
  • CAT-72 Evidence: Successful completion of 72+ hour convergence test
  • Architectural Consonance: Training data and safety envelope derived from common simulation

2. Applicant Obligations

2.1 Application Requirements

Applicant shall:

  • Provide complete and accurate application materials
  • Disclose all relevant system specifications and operational parameters
  • Identify all boundaries, tolerances, and constraints in the ODD specification
  • Submit documentation of ENVELO-compliant enforcement architecture

2.2 Testing Requirements

Applicant shall:

  • Deploy the ENVELO Agent as configured by SA
  • Conduct CAT-72 testing under normal operational conditions
  • Not modify system configuration, ODD parameters, or enforcement settings during testing
  • Report any test interruptions, violations, or anomalies

2.3 Post-Certification Requirements

Upon certification, Applicant shall:

  • Maintain ENVELO Agent in continuous operation during certified system operation
  • Not bypass, disable, or tamper with enforcement mechanisms
  • Notify SA of any system modifications affecting ODD or enforcement
  • Complete renewal testing prior to conformance expiration
  • Use the ODDC mark only in accordance with mark usage guidelines

3. SA Obligations

SA shall:

  • Review applications within stated timelines
  • Conduct scope assessment and provide fee quotation
  • Administer CAT-72 testing per documented procedures
  • Issue conformance determination based on evidentiary record
  • Maintain certification records per retention requirements
  • Provide certificate verification services

What SA Does Not Do

SA does not:

  • Implement, operate, or monitor runtime systems
  • Certify implementation correctness or validate code
  • Warrant system behavior or performance
  • Provide regulatory approval or compliance certification
  • Assume any operational responsibility for certified systems

4. Conformance Terms

4.1 Term Duration

Conformance terms range from 6-24 months based on operational domain risk profile, ODD complexity, and applicant conformance history. Term duration is specified in the conformance determination.

4.2 Renewal

Renewal requires CAT-72 re-completion. Streamlined renewal (48-hour test duration) may be available for applicants with clean conformance history and no modifications.

4.3 Modifications

Any modification to the certified system's ODD specification, enforcement architecture, or underlying simulation requires:

  • Notification to SA within 5 business days
  • Evaluation of modification impact
  • Potential re-certification if modification affects conformance basis

5. Revocation

SA may revoke conformance for:

  • Material misrepresentation in application materials
  • Unauthorized modification to certified system
  • Disabling, bypassing, or tampering with ENVELO enforcement
  • Failure to maintain ENVELO Agent operation
  • Non-payment of fees
  • Violation of this Agreement

Revocation is recorded in the SA registry and reflected in verification results.

6. Liability and Indemnification

6.1 Limitation of Liability

SA's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the fees paid for the specific certification at issue. SA is not liable for indirect, incidental, consequential, special, or punitive damages.

6.2 No Liability for System Behavior

SA has no liability for the behavior, performance, or consequences of operation of any system, whether certified or not. Certification attests to bounded operation at time of determination; it does not guarantee future behavior.

6.3 Indemnification

Applicant shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless SA from any claims, damages, losses, or expenses arising from: operation of Applicant's systems; Applicant's violation of this Agreement; claims by third parties related to Applicant's products or services.

7. Confidentiality

SA will maintain confidentiality of Applicant's technical documentation and application materials, except:

  • Certificate information is publicly verifiable
  • Disclosure required by law or valid legal process
  • Aggregate, anonymized data for research or reporting

8. Intellectual Property

Applicant retains all rights to its systems, documentation, and intellectual property. SA retains all rights to ODDC methodology, ENVELO specifications, CAT-72 procedures, and related intellectual property. Nothing in this Agreement grants either party rights to the other's intellectual property except as expressly stated.

9.2 Entire Agreement

This Agreement, together with the Terms of Service and applicable fee schedules, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.

9.3 Amendments

9.4 Severability
