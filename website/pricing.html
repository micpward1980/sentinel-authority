Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the setup fee?
The one-time setup fee covers your initial ODDC certification process: application review, ODD documentation analysis, ENVELO Agent configuration, CAT-72 test coordination, and certificate issuance. This is a one-time cost when you first certify a system.
What happens if my system fails CAT-72?
If your system fails the initial CAT-72 test, we'll provide detailed analysis of the violations and work with you to address them. You can re-run CAT-72 at no additional cost within 90 days of your initial setup.
Can I add more systems later?
Yes! You can add additional systems at any time. Each additional system requires its own CAT-72 certification ($5,000 per system) and increases your monitoring tier if you exceed your plan's system limit.
What if the ENVELO Agent detects a violation?
The ENVELO Agent blocks any action that violates your declared ODD boundaries in real-time. Violations are logged and reported to your dashboard. Repeated or severe violations may trigger a certification review to ensure your system remains conformant.
How long does certification take?
Typical timeline is 2-4 weeks: 1 week for application review and setup, then 72 hours for the CAT-72 test, followed by certificate issuance within 48 hours of a passing test.
What's the contract term?
Monitoring plans are billed annually. You can cancel at renewal, but note that your ODDC certification requires active ENVELO Agent monitoring — if monitoring lapses, your certification is suspended until monitoring resumes.
