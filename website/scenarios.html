Data Centers & Hyperscale Computing

Hyperscale environments increasingly rely on autonomous systems to allocate power, optimize cooling, place workloads, and orchestrate AI-on-AI stacks. ODDC conformance scenarios illustrate facility-level and stack-level envelope boundaries (power draw, thermal ceilings, isolation requirements, recursion limits), enforced via ENVELO-compliant non-bypassable runtime interlocks.

Representative interlock behavior (modeled)

  • Hard-cap interlocks on CPU throttling to prevent TDP overruns during load spikes
  • Automatic workload shedding to non-critical queues as facility power approaches envelope limits
  • Maximum recursion depth for AI managing AI without human checkpoint

Evidence outputs

  • Append-only envelope adherence logs
  • Cryptographic attestations suitable for underwriting review and incident reconstruction
