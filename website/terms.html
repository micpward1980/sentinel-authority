07Continuous Surveillance & Automated Enforcement

Attested systems are subject to continuous automated surveillance throughout the Conformance Term. By obtaining an attestation, you acknowledge and consent to the following monitoring and enforcement mechanisms.

7.1 — Heartbeat Monitoring

The ENVELO Interlock must maintain an active connection to the Sentinel Authority platform. Heartbeat signals are transmitted at regular intervals to confirm operational status. Failure to maintain connectivity triggers the escalation thresholds described in Section 7.3.

7.2 — Telemetry and Violation Tracking

All boundary evaluations performed by the ENVELO Interlock are recorded as telemetry. Each action is classified as PASS (within operational envelope) or BLOCK (boundary violation prevented). Violation rates are calculated and monitored continuously.

7.3 — Automated Enforcement Thresholds

The following automated enforcement actions are applied without prior notice:

Warning: ENVELO Interlock offline for more than 5 minutes triggers a warning alert
Automatic Suspension: Offline for more than 24 consecutive hours results in automatic suspension. The public registry status displays “SUSPENDED” and the attestation is not valid during suspension
Automatic Revocation: Offline for more than 72 consecutive hours results in permanent revocation. Revocation is irreversible — a new application and full CAT-72 assessment are required
Violation-Rate Suspension: Violation rate exceeding 20% (after a minimum of 100 recorded actions) results in automatic suspension

7.4 — Grace Period

A two-hour grace period applies following platform restarts to prevent false enforcement actions caused by infrastructure maintenance.

7.5 — Reinstatement

Suspended attestations may be reinstated upon restoration of ENVELO Interlock operation and satisfactory review by Sentinel Authority. Reinstatement is not guaranteed. Revoked attestations cannot be reinstated.

7.6 — Notifications

Sentinel Authority will make reasonable efforts to notify you by email of enforcement actions. However, enforcement actions take effect immediately and are not contingent upon successful delivery of notification.
