Assessment Includes
  • 01
    Pre-CAT-72 Audit Control Review
    Formal evaluation of ODD boundary definition, ENVELO Interlock assurance configuration, and telemetry readiness. Systems that do not meet the audit threshold are returned with findings before entering the verification period.
  • 02
    CAT-72 Verification Period
    72 cumulative hours of system operation under active ENVELO Interlock assurance within the declared ODD. Cryptographic audit chain maintained across all operational intervals.
  • 03
    Conformance Determination & Certificate Issuance
    Sentinel Authority reviews telemetry records, validates audit chain integrity, and issues a conformance determination recorded in the public registry.
  • 04
    Continuous Post-Certification Surveillance
    Conformance status remains valid only while assurance remains active. Certified systems are subject to continuous conformance surveillance included in annual maintenance.
