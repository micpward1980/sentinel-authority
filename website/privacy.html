Privacy Policy

Effective: January 2026

Sentinel Authority ("we," "us," or "our") operates the sentinelauthority.org website and the ODDC certification platform. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Contact information (name, email, phone, organization)
  • Application data (system descriptions, ODD specifications, technical documentation)
  • Account credentials for the certification portal
  • Payment information (processed by third-party providers)

Information collected automatically:

  • ENVELO Agent telemetry data from systems undergoing certification
  • Log data (IP address, browser type, pages visited)
  • Cookies for session management

How We Use Your Information

  • Process and evaluate certification applications
  • Conduct CAT-72 conformance testing
  • Issue and manage ODDC certificates
  • Communicate about your application or certification status
  • Maintain audit records as required for certification integrity
  • Improve our services and platform

Data Retention

We retain certification records, including CAT-72 evidentiary data, for the conformance term plus seven (7) years, as required by our certification procedures. This retention is necessary for audit purposes and incident reconstruction.

Account information is retained while your account is active. You may request deletion of non-certification data by contacting us.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information:

  • With service providers who assist our operations (hosting, email, payment processing)
  • To comply with legal obligations or valid legal process
  • To protect our rights or the safety of others
  • With your consent

Certificate information (certificate number, organization name, system name, conformance status) is publicly verifiable through our verification portal.

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit and at rest, access controls, and regular security assessments.

Your Rights

You may:

  • Access and receive a copy of your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of non-certification data
  • Opt out of marketing communications

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy on our website with a new effective date.
